Quick Veggie Stir-Fry

Quick and healthy stir-fry with seasonal vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Stir in soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Serve hot.
Spicy Chickpea Curry

A hearty and spicy chickpea curry that's easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in a pan until soft.
  2. Add curry powder and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add chickpeas and coconut milk, simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and serve with rice.
Mediterranean Quinoa Salad

A refreshing salad packed with Mediterranean flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine quinoa, cucumber, tomatoes, and feta.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  3. Toss to combine and serve chilled.
Garlic Lemon Chicken

Juicy chicken marinated in garlic and lemon.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Marinate chicken for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Grill or bake chicken until cooked through.
  4. Serve with vegetables or salad.
Vegetable Fried Rice

A quick and easy fried rice loaded with vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan and scramble the eggs.
  2. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add cooked rice and soy sauce, stir-fry until heated through.
  4. Serve hot.
Caprese Salad

A classic Italian salad with fresh mozzarella and tomatoes.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 ball fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Layer tomato and mozzarella slices on a plate.

  3. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar and season with salt and pepper.

Sweet Potato Mash

A creamy and healthy alternative to mashed potatoes.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Boil sweet potatoes until tender, about 15 minutes.
  2. Drain and mash with butter, milk, salt, and pepper.
  3. Serve warm.
Banana Oatmeal Cookies

Healthy cookies made with bananas and oats.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup oats
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until combined.
  3. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden.
Lentil Soup

A hearty and nutritious lentil soup.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onion and carrots in a pot until soft.
  2. Add lentils, broth, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Serve hot.
Chocolate Avocado Mousse

A rich and creamy dessert made with avocados.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Blend all ingredients in a food processor until smooth.
  2. Chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  3. Serve with fresh berries on top.
Zucchini Noodles with Pesto

A light and healthy alternative to pasta using zucchini noodles.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium zucchinis, spiralized
  • 1/2 cup pesto sauce
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add zucchini noodles and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Stir in pesto sauce and cherry tomatoes.
  4. Cook for another 2 minutes, season with salt and pepper, and serve.
Beef Tacos

Flavorful beef tacos topped with fresh ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook ground beef in a pan until browned, drain excess fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water, simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Fill taco shells with beef and top with lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese.
  4. Serve immediately.
Chickpea Salad Sandwich

A protein-packed sandwich made with chickpeas and veggies.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and mashed
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, diced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices whole grain bread

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix chickpeas, mayonnaise, celery, onion, salt, and pepper.
  2. Spread mixture onto whole grain bread.
  3. Top with additional veggies if desired, then close the sandwich.
  4. Serve immediately.
Baked Salmon with Dill

Healthy and flavorful salmon baked with fresh dill.

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place salmon on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with dill.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, then bake for 12-15 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with a side of vegetables.
Vegan Chili

A hearty chili packed with beans and vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cups diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onion and bell pepper in a pot until soft.
  2. Add black beans, kidney beans, tomatoes, and chili powder.
  3. Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Serve hot with your favorite toppings.

A refreshing smoothie made with ripe mangoes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe mango, diced

Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Crispy roasted Brussels sprouts with balsamic glaze.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Brussels sprouts, halved
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Toss Brussels sprouts with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes.
  4. Drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.
Pasta Primavera

A colorful pasta dish loaded with fresh vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta of choice
  • 1 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions, drain.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté vegetables until tender.
  3. Add cooked pasta, salt, and pepper, toss to combine.
  4. Serve hot with Parmesan cheese if desired.
